# Clinical Scenario: Seborrheic Dermatitis

## Overview
Seborrheic dermatitis is a chronic condition with optimization through comorbidity detection.

---

## E/M Level
| Scenario | E/M | Justification |
|----------|-----|---------------|
| Single site, stable | 99213 | Chronic stable |
| Multiple sites | 99213-99214 | Multiple areas |
| Psoriasis overlap | 99214 | Multiple conditions |

## G2211: Always Add
Chronic condition requiring ongoing management. +0.33 wRVU.

---

## Comorbidities

| Finding | Opportunity |
|---------|-------------|
| Psoriasis overlap ("sebopsoriasis") | Bill for both conditions |
| Scalp + face + chest | Multiple sites increases MDM |
| Resistant thick plaques | IL injection (11900) |
| Tinea differential | KOH prep |
| Rosacea overlap | Second diagnosis |

---
