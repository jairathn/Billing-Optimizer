# Clinical Scenario: Injection Visit

## Overview
Injection visits include IL steroids, biologics administration, and chemotherapy agents.

---

## Intralesional Steroid (11900/11901)

| Lesion Count | Code | wRVU |
|--------------|------|------|
| 1-7 | 11900 | 0.51 |
| 8+ | 11901 | 0.78 |

### Indications
- Keloids/hypertrophic scars
- Alopecia areata
- Inflammatory cysts
- Psoriatic plaques
- Granuloma annulare
- Lichen simplex chronicus

---

## Chemotherapy Injection (96405/96406)

| Lesion Count | Code | wRVU |
|--------------|------|------|
| 1-7 | 96405 | 0.52 |
| 8+ | 96406 | 0.80 |

### Agents
- 5-Fluorouracil (warts, keratoses)
- Bleomycin (warts)

**Key:** Chemo agents use 96405/96406, NOT 11900!

---

## Biologic Administration (96372)

**Code:** 96372 (0.17 wRVU) + J-code for drug

### Common J-Codes
- Dupixent, Humira, Stelara, etc.
- Bill administration + drug

---

## Billing Examples

### 5 Keloids Injected with TAC
- 99213-25 + 11900 + G2211 = 2.14 wRVU

### 10 AA Patches Injected
- 99214-25 + 11901 + G2211 = 3.03 wRVU

### 6 Warts with IL 5-FU
- 99213-25 + 96405 + J-code = 1.82 wRVU + drug

### Biologic Administration
- 99214 + 96372 + J-code + G2211 = 2.42 wRVU + drug

---

## Documentation

> "Intralesional [triamcinolone X mg/mL / 5-FU / bleomycin] injected into [number] [lesion type] at [locations]. Total volume: [X] mL."

---

## "Next Time" Points
- COUNT every lesion for 11900/11901 or 96405/96406
- Chemotherapy agents = 96405/96406, not 11900
- Bill drug (J-code) separately
- G2211 for chronic condition management
