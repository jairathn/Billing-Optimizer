# Clinical Scenario: Contact Dermatitis

## Overview
Contact dermatitis offers patch testing opportunities and management of secondary complications.

---

## E/M Level
| Scenario | E/M | Justification |
|----------|-----|---------------|
| Acute, single site | 99213 | Low complexity |
| Chronic/recurrent | 99214 | Needs workup |
| Occupational | 99214 | Higher complexity, documentation |
| Multiple sites | 99214 | Multiple areas affected |

## G2211
Add for chronic/recurrent contact dermatitis requiring ongoing management.

---

## Procedure Opportunities

### Patch Testing (95044)
- Per allergen - standard panels 80+ allergens
- Major revenue opportunity
- Multiple visits for reading

### Photo Patch Testing (95052)
- For photoallergic contact dermatitis
- Sun-exposed distribution

---

## Comorbidities

| Finding | Opportunity |
|---------|-------------|
| Secondary infection | Higher MDM, separate Rx |
| Chronic/recurrent | Patch testing indication |
| Occupational | Documentation, complexity |
| Atopic dermatitis overlap | Multiple diagnoses |

---

## Billing Examples

### Acute Contact Dermatitis
- 99213 = 1.30 wRVU

### Chronic, Patch Testing Indicated
- 99214 + G2211 = 2.25 wRVU
- Patch testing visits: significant additional revenue
